About noidea

noidea is a Git companion that makes your commits better and funnier. It was created to bring a touch of personality and humor to the often serious world of version control. With noidea, you get smart commit message suggestions based on your changes, sassy feedback from a judgmental Moai after each commit, and insightful analysis of your Git history.

The tool embraces the chaos of coding, not trying to fix your Git hygiene completely, but rather making it more entertaining while still improving the quality of your commit messages. Whether you're a solo developer or part of a team, noidea can help make your Git workflow more enjoyable and productive.
